**[[redemption-elimination|The elimination of discretionary redemption risk]]**

The legacy real estate industry relies on commingled funds that expose investors
to severe liquidity crises during market downturns. The Woodfine Direct-Hold
Solution fundamentally restructures this dynamic by mathematically eliminating
discretionary redemption risk.

By tying investor equity directly to an isolated, single-purpose asset with its
own verifiable {{gli|Site Ledger}}, the model ensures that capital cannot be
diluted or re-allocated to cover the systemic failures of unrelated portfolios.
This structural isolation provides institutional investors with a mathematically
sound, fiduciary-first vehicle.
