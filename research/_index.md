Research covers institutional market intelligence relevant to direct-hold real estate
investment. Articles in this category synthesize factual findings from financial media
and academic research on private markets, real estate capital flows, demographic trends,
and regional economic conditions. Research summaries present the evidentiary context for
investment in direct-hold commercial real estate — sourced and cited, not reproduced.
