**Last updated:** June 2026

This wiki records a single technical measurement each time a page is viewed: the URL path of the page and the approximate time of the visit. No name, email address, IP address, browser fingerprint, advertising identifier, or cookie is collected or stored. No data is shared with third parties. No advertising networks are involved. No tracking scripts from outside this server are loaded.

The measurement is used only to understand which topics are read and at what frequency, so that the editorial team can prioritise content. It is retained for a limited period and is not linked to any identity.

You may visit this wiki without restriction regardless of whether you accept or decline the measurement described above.

For questions about this notice, contact us at open.source@pointsav.com.
